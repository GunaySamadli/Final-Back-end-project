Q: How is this bracelet made of solid gold but be hollow?
Answers (1)
Mejuri
03/15/21
A: Hi there! Happy to help! Solid simply means the piece is made of gold inside and out - there's no plating here. As 100% pure gold is too soft for everyday wear, we use a standard 14k alloy which contains 58.5% gold! While made of solid gold, our Flat Curb Chain is also hollow, which means there is an empty space inside the links, making it super lightweight for your everyday wear!

Measure Your Wrist

Bracelet sizing Guide

Not sure about size? Here are two ways to find your wrist size.

Option 1

Measuring Tape

With your palm open and facing up, wrap the measuring tape around your wrist just above the wrist bone.

Note down the measurement of your wrist.

Option 2

String Method

  1. With your palm open and facing up, wrap the measuring tape around your wrist just above the wrist bone.

  2. Mark where the ends meet with a pen or marker.

  3. Lay the string flat and measure it to where you’ve made your mark and note down the measurement to find your wrist sizer.
